Mission Intel ‚Äî FAQ
Do I need animal experience?
Not at all! The training process is designed for complete beginners. All you need is enthusiasm and a love for animals.
Can I switch departments?
Absolutely. Just email the volunteer team and they'll get you transferred. Note that each department has its own onboarding process.
How long before I hear back?
Usually within a week. If you haven't heard anything, check your spam folder then follow up with the team directly.
Can under-16s get involved?
Younger individuals may be able to participate through group volunteering with a school or organization. Contact the team to explore options.
Is there a max hours limit?
No maximum! Volunteer as often as your schedule allows. Just meet the 4-hour monthly minimum and you're good to go.
What if I can't meet the monthly minimum?
Life happens! Just communicate with the volunteer team in advance. Keeping them informed protects your volunteer status.
What is MyImpact?
It's the volunteer management platform (also called BetterImpact) where you apply, sign documents, complete training, and schedule shifts. Your account is created when you apply.
Do I need to set up Shelterluv?
Yes! Set it up before your first shift for smooth check-in. It's also used for on-site T-shirt purchases (cash & card accepted).
